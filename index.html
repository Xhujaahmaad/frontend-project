Bussiness Registration Application Process

  • Click the Register A Business button.
  • Answer a few basic questions (Name, Address, Name of Business, Owner /Partners / Directors details etc.)
  • Indicate the type of Business structure that you'd like to start: Sole Trader,Partnership, Limited Liability Company.
  • Your name search form is automatically generated and used to search a list of companies in Trinidad and Tobago at the Ministry of Legal Affairs - Companies Registry.
  • The business registration form, registration form for partnerships or company egistration form is automatically filled out using the information that you entered.
  • After Checkout, your Business Name would be reserved and registration form submitted.
  • An Application Manager would begin working on your Order within 24 hours / 1 Business day of checkout.
  • Forms that require a physical signature can be mailed in, dropped off, done in person,or collected via our courier (Only if you choose to use our courier for signature collection would there be an additional cost.)
  • Your final package, including your Certificate of Registration, can be collected or delivered to you for FREE.
