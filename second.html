What Is Your Business About?

We recommend a made-up name combined with a description of what you do. For example, our business is named "advance business solutions Application Services".

Choose a Unique and Distinctive name. Your chosen name should allow others to tell you apart from other businesses, without any confusion.It is strongly recommended that the person filling out this application (YOU) be the primary contact.

You cannot include words that are reserved for Government use. eg. "Ministry", "National", "Army", "Police", "Caricom", "Bank", "Commonwealth" etc.
Your name must not include "CO-OPERATIVE", "CO-OP", "CREDIT UNION" or "FRIENDLY SOCIETY".

Business Names should contain multiple words. A single-word business name is often rejected.
